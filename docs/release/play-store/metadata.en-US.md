## Full Description (Primary)
Sports Trivia is a competitive quiz game for sports fans who want fast rounds, social competition, and long-term progression.

Play across sports topics, answer timed questions, and improve your rank with every attempt. Challenge friends directly, compare results, and keep your streak alive.

What you can do:
- Play quick sports quizzes across multiple topics
- Track your progress with streaks, badges, and profile stats
- Compete on leaderboards and improve your standing
- Send and respond to friend challenges
- Discover new quizzes by topic and difficulty

Built for repeat play:
- Smooth quiz flow with clear answer feedback
- Progress and history visible from your profile dashboard
- Social features to keep competition active

Privacy and account controls:
- In-app account deletion is available in Profile -> Settings -> Danger Zone
- Optional account data export is available before deletion

Whether you play solo or with friends, Sports Trivia gives you a fast, competitive way to test and improve your sports knowledge.
